# Product Operating System – Summary

## Overview

Organizations often struggle to connect strategy, product execution, and continuous improvement into a cohesive system.

The Product Operating System (POS) provides a simple model to align these functions, enabling teams to deliver measurable outcomes while continuously improving how they work.

---

## The Model

The Product Operating System is built on four integrated components:

1. **Product Value Creation**  
   Idea → Discovery → Delivery → Outcomes  
   Focuses teams on solving validated customer problems and delivering measurable value.

2. **Strategy Alignment**  
   Strategy → Objectives → Priorities → Backlog  
   Translates strategic intent into actionable work through clear prioritization.

3. **Continuous Improvement**
